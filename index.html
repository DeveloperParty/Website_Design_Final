Who are the Chicago Cubs?

The Chicago Cubs are a major league baseball team based out of Chicago, IL. They play in the national league and have won three World Series titles. Winning in 1907, 1908, and recently in 2016.
They have been a team since 1876 and used to be known as the White Stockings. Impressivly they even set a record in 1906 that still holds up today as the best winning percentage in basball history with a 76% winrate.

"Our goal - and it was a goal we laid out a few years ago - was to try to be more consistent" is a direct quote from Tom Ricketts, who is the chairman of the Chicago Mets.

This update comes at a surprise of many since the Cubs have made it to postseason five out of the past six seasons.
